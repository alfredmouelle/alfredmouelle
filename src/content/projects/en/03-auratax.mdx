# AuraTax — file your returns, the right way

## The product

**AuraTax** is a SaaS platform built by **AuraSoft Inc.** that helps Canadian businesses **prepare and transmit their corporation tax returns**. It positions itself as a thoughtful, reliable solution that handles federal and provincial tax specifics and is designed to transmit a return in minutes.

> *"File your returns, the right way."* — AuraTax's promise.

## Key features

- **Guided preparation** — Assisted entry of corporation tax returns, with validation and compliance checks.
- **Online transmission** — Return transmission compliant with Canada Revenue Agency and provincial requirements.
- **Desktop app** — A desktop version available on macOS and Windows, alongside the web app.
- **Data security** — Robust authentication, input validation and encryption of sensitive tax data.

## Tech stack

- **Backend**: Java Spring Boot, for robust and scalable APIs (user management, returns, complex calculations).
- **Frontend**: Angular and TypeScript, for a responsive and accessible interface.
- **Database**: MongoDB, to store and manipulate large volumes of tax data.
- **E2E testing**: Cypress (previously Protractor), ensuring the stability of critical features.
- **Desktop**: an app packaged for macOS and Windows.

## My role

I joined AuraSoft Inc. in February 2022 as a **Full-Stack Developer**, contributing to both the backend and the frontend of the platform. In February 2024 I was promoted to **Team Lead**: I now supervise a team of developers while continuing to evolve the product.

My contributions span performance optimization (MongoDB queries and backend architecture to absorb large data volumes), tax-data security, and continuous improvement of the Angular interface based on user feedback.
